This is oven-bot! He is equipped to preheat the oven, set a timer, and turn the oven light on and off. Give him a few tasks like these:

  • "Set the oven to 300 degrees"
  • "Turn off the oven"
  • "Turn the oven light on"
  • "Turn off the oven light"
  • "Set timer for 10 minutes"
